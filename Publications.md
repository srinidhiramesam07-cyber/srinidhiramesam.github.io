# Publications

## Bonded Labour and its Existence in Different Sectors in India  
**Journal:** International Journal of Novel Research and Development (IJNRD)  

This paper examines the persistence of bonded labour practices across various sectors in India. It analyses the constitutional safeguards, statutory protections, and judicial interpretations addressing forced labour and exploitation. The study highlights implementation challenges and suggests reforms for stronger enforcement mechanisms.

---

## Legal Issues on Patent Law in Pharmaceutical Companies  
**Journal:** International Journal of Novel Research and Development (IJNRD)  

This research focuses on patent law challenges within the pharmaceutical industry. It explores issues relating to compulsory licensing, evergreening, public health concerns, and the balance between innovation and accessibility. The paper critically analyses statutory provisions and landmark judicial decisions.
